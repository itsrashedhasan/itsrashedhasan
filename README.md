## 🧠 What I Do

I build AI systems, full-stack applications, mobile apps, and IoT solutions — and research the hard problems in between. My work sits at the intersection of **medical AI**, **federated learning**, and **real-world software engineering**.

- 🔬 **Researcher** — First author of 2 accepted international papers (Springer LNNS · IEEE). Active thesis on privacy-preserving federated learning for cardiac MRI segmentation.
- 💻 **Developer** — 16+ projects across web, mobile, AI/ML, embedded systems, and computer graphics.
- 🤝 **Community** — Research lab organizer, Python instructor, hackathon builder, programming contest participant.
- 🌍 **Open Source** — All academic and personal projects shared publicly on GitHub.
